Aerogel Experimentation
As part of my Materials Science education, I was on a team that studied a relatively new type of material: aerogels. We studied the material properties, manufacturing processes, and environmental effects of two types of aerogels: holmium oxide aerogel and silica aerogel.

In our project, we also manufactured our own aerogel samples using various procedures, and commented on various aspects of the differing processes and results. This project involved a lot of time in the lab working with various machines and chemicals, and necessitated that my team be trained in handling various hazardous materials.
